Coffee App

Overview

When we were asked to design a mobile app for a coffee company of our choosing, my first thought was to create an online ordering application for my friend’s historic tea company.

Although Oliver Pluff & Co. is quite literally steeped in history, I thought the company’s branding could use some updating. It was a ton of fun combining the old and the new on this UI assignment.

Wireframes

In his bestselling book, The Tipping Point, Malcom Gladwell describes how the best way to make an idea, movement, or product “sticky,” is to make its message interactive in some way.

I knew going into this project that transforming the buying process into a game might encourage users to return to the app more often to purchase Oliver Pluff & Co. products.
