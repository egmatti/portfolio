Coffee App

Overview

When we were asked to design a mobile app for a coffee company of our choosing, my first thought was to create an online ordering application for my friend’s historic tea company.

Although Oliver Pluff & Co. is quite literally steeped in history, I thought the company’s branding could use some updating. It was a ton of fun combining the old and the new on this UI assignment.
